My Internet Footprint

From a young age, I've always been taught that the way you present yourself online is super important. I have always tried to maintain a positive view of myself online and I think I've done a pretty good job at this (compared to others I know). I make videos sometimes on TikTok, YouTube and Twitch. I don't really want to become famous or anything, but it's just fun to hang out with friends and maybe even meet new people. The only thing that really comes up when I search my name is some mother-son wedding song(?) and news articles about myself when I was younger. When I lived in Trail and visited my dad in Vernon, there was never really any stories in such a small, uneventful place. I got on the Vernon Morning Star paper once when I was four because I was feeding some seagulls french fries on Kin Beach, and another time on the Trail Daily Times when I was playing with my childhood friend at the local park. I think it would be cool if one day I could establish myself more online, but for now there isn't really much out there about me.
